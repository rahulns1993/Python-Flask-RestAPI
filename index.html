The Avengers

The Avengers are Earth's mightiest heroes, brought together to combat the threats that no single hero could withstand. Led by Captain America, the team includes Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye. They have fought numerous battles to protect the planet from extraterrestrial invaders, mad titans, and rogue AI. Their bravery, teamwork, and unyielding spirit have made them legends, inspiring countless individuals around the globe to stand up for what is right.
